Why APNC Rated ?


Interviewers constantly assess existing Manpower and Candidates on Behavioral Dimensions and Value Systems.


An Assessment which helps quantify universally applicable dimensions without barriers of geography, industry or job size is a compelling aid for manpower planning and recruitment processes.


Any interview process is intrinsically influenced by external factors, like environment, and is based on the interviewer’s capacity to assess objectively; the results might not always be objective and unbiased.


APNC Rated offers a reliable tool to the interviewer and lends uniformity to the interviewing process.
